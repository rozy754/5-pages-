Resolution of uncooperative law enforcement officers.

When faced with uncooperative police who refuse to register a complaint, there are several steps one can take:

1. Send the complaint by post to the relevant police station.
2. Lodge a complaint with the Superintendent of Police regarding the refusal to register the FIR. If the Superintendent of Police fails to take action, one can alternatively approach the magistrate of the area under Section 156 of the Code of Criminal Procedure to ensure that the FIR is registered
