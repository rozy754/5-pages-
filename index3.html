Livein Rights:

1.Rights for women in case men seperate after live in relationship :
Though live-in relationship is no where defined in legislation but it is the supreme court guide lines which are considered in this case .
supreme court in a landmark case has declared that three prerequisite conditions needs to be fulfilled for a couple to be considered in a live-in relationship.
(i) The couple should show to the society that they are living as husband and wife.
(ii) The relationship should be continued for a considerable amount of time.
(iii) The result of relation should be procreation . if all the above mentioned conditions are fulfilled then the girl will be given all the rights as a divorced women.
